About GlobalMart

Founded in 2018 to connect buyers worldwide with the most authentic products from every corner of the globe.

Our Story

From a Single Idea to a Global Marketplace

GlobalMart was founded in 2018 by five travelers who were frustrated by the difficulty of finding authentic products from around the world. They believed a handmade rug from Morocco or a silk scarf from Varanasi deserved to reach homes everywhere — without the buyer having to travel to get it.

Starting with 200 products from 12 countries, we have grown into a platform connecting over 4,000 independent artisans and certified suppliers with millions of customers across 120+ countries.

Our mission: bring the world to your door.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Fair Trade

Every supplier receives fair compensation. We conduct annual audits to maintain ethical standards across our supplier network.

Sustainability

All packaging is 100 percent recyclable. We offset shipping carbon emissions through certified reforestation projects.

Authenticity

Every product is verified by our curation team before listing. We do not accept counterfeits or misrepresented goods under any circumstances.

Cultural Respect

We celebrate the origins of every product and ensure cultural heritage is credited and honoured throughout our platform.
